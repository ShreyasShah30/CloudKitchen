2. Meals cannot be carried forward beyond the specified days.
3. Dispatched meals will not be cancelled, returned & refunded under any circumstances.

Delivery and Other Terms
  • Our Delivery is done through mumbai's Dabbawalas Or borzo /dunzo, In unforeseen circumstances such as riots, heavy rains, or natural disasters, strikes by dabbawalas, Dabbawalas going on annual leave etc. delivery may be affected. You will be notified of any changes in delivery schedules, All meals cancelled by us will be carried forwarded automatically.
  • Dhunkee's delivery partners would drop the customer's meal at the reception/security gate/Common area in case if they are not allowed to enter the building premises.
  • In order to avoid spillage, the meals are cooled down at room temperature and then packed in microwave safe containers. Therefore customers may receive food at room temperature. It is recommended that in order to enjoy the meals, customers must re-heat the food before consumption.
  • At Dhunkee's, we put in a lot of effort to ensure that your order reaches you on time and in an ideal condition. However, due to unforeseen events your order may reach you late and in rare cases may be undelivered. In such a circumstance, please contact the delivery guys or contact us. We assure you that every attempt will be made to rectify the situation or compensate you.
